Welcome to RoadFix

Building Safer Roads Through Community Action

We combine citizen reporting, transparent tracking, and faster response to fix potholes, drains, and road hazards before they become bigger risks.

Our Mission

RoadFix represents the future of civic technology. We believe that safe roads and functional infrastructure lead directly to thriving communities. By empowering citizens with an easy-to-use reporting tool, we streamline communication with maintenance crews and city officials—ensuring that potholes sink, broken lights shine, and hazards are handled efficiently.
