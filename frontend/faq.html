Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about tracking, reporting, and utilizing RoadFix in your community.

How do I report a road issue?
Navigate to "Report Issue", fill the form, drop a map pin, and submit.
Can I track my report status?
Yes, each report gets a tracking ID. Check the dashboard anytime.
Who receives my reports?
Reports are visible to all admins who manage resolution.
Is my personal data safe?
We only collect location and issue data needed to fix the problem. Private identity markers are not public.
How long does it take to resolve?
Depends on category. Blocked drains: 3 days, potholes: 7 days typically based on SLAs.
Can I report anonymously?
Currently we require a citizen login for accountability, but no public ID is shown on the dashboard publicly.
What areas does RoadFix cover?
RoadFix is a platform configured per municipality. Usually, city limits and surrounding suburbs are fully covered.
How do I contact support?
Use the Contact page form or email our helpdesk at support@roadfix.com directly.
